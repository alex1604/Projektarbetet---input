About the company

West Shades is a company that wants to change how people look. It was founded by four people of different backgrounds. With influences from different places in Sweden and from Spain. West Shades takes inspiration and influences from more than the local traditions in the west coast of Sweden.

Company Vision

As mentioned West Shades wants to change how people look. And we want to deliver a highend product that stuns people with it's looks. We want to do this with a foundation on the west coast of Sweden with an ambition to spread the mixture of Spanish and Swedish design globally.
